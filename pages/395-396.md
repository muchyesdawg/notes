	- normal process in which homologous chromatids exchange genetic material. increases variation of genetic material.

- cyclosis
	- movement of cytoplasm around the cell
- cystic fibrosis

- cytochromes

- cytokinesis
	- division of the cytoplasm. In animal cells, a cleavage furrow forms down the middle of the cell as the cytoplasm pinches inward and the two daughter cells seperate from each other.
